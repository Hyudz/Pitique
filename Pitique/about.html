About Pitique

Pitique is derived from the Filipino word "pitik" which means "to flick." However, the younger generations use pitik as a slang word to refer to a photograph or to be photographed. Basically the word "Pitique" is a Fitnessed version of the slang word. Our teams aims to develop a website that serves as a hub for us to create, share, and learn more about the art of photography. We believe with this initiative, we will be able to inspire and encourage others to appreciate every aspect of life which is embodied through photography.
